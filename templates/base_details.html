I have such a deep respect for the uniqueness of each family. I love to capture spontaneous, irreverent, joyful, intangible moments that accentuate what makes YOUR family special.

All sessions are done on location either outdoors or in and around your home. Children and families are most relaxed in environments that feel friendly!

Your session will be a real mix of documenting your relationships, the fun, tenderness, teasing , laughing, contemplating, all the emotions that are part of the time and place.

Sometimes I will ask you to get together for a lightly posed shot,. Always it is about your connection to each other and not about me asking you to look into the camera.
